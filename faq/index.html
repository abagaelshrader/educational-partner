Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this good for?

You are a parent or guardian of a K-12 student and have:

  • Needed to move learning home LONG-TERM due to COVID-19 concerns.
  • Required to move learning home TEMPORARILY due to COVID-19 isolation.
  • Recently decided to explore alternative learning from home IN ADDITION to regular school.

How does this work?

You (parent/guardian) provides me with information about you and your student, including their strengths and interests. We discuss their learning style and your goals for this time at home. From there, I develop a personalized learning plan with specific goals, resources, lessons, and assessments. You start to implement them and stay in contact if things need changed or added.

Can I work with you outside of packages?

Yes! The packages are provided for easy bundling, but add-ons are available as well. Schedule a 15 minute FREE consultation and we can discuss what works best for you and your student.

Can't I figure this out on my own?

Yup! You are 100% able and capable of doing this on your own. But you may not want to, or don’t have the time. That’s where I come into the picture.

Is this tutoring?

No. While I love working directly with students, my goal is to empower and support parents.

Do I have to use the materials you provide?

NOPE! If you are finding that more than a couple of ideas fall short, just contact me and we can figure it out! You know your student best, and I have certainly learned as a teacher that sometimes a lesson just fails. We will work together to find a plan and resources that work for you.
