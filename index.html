From the U.S. to China, from higher education to K-12 public education, from urban to rural schools - we all want one thing - for our students to have the best education in the safest possible environment. I want to help.

I'm Abagael. I have worked with teachers, students, and parents for over 5 years. Education is my passion and I want all students to experience challenge and engagement in their learning. Not only is this challenging in our regular underfunded and overcrowded classrooms, it’s even more difficult when our students are sent home. State and national standards are hard to understand, digital resources are overly plentiful, expectations are high, and time is limited.

With teaching endorsements in Social Studies, Computer Science, and Talented and Gifted, I work with parents and students of all ages to develop learning plans that are personalized to their individual needs and interests. If you are interested in learning more, please schedule a 15 minute FREE consultation to talk about you and your students’ needs.
